# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in HyprSequoia a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education,
socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or
sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Be empathetic, respectful, constructive, and accountable. Unacceptable conduct
includes harassment, sexualized attention, personal or political attacks,
trolling, publishing private information, and conduct inappropriate in a
professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the project maintainers through the
security contact listed on the GitHub repository. Maintainers will investigate
fairly, respect reporter privacy, and may correct, warn, temporarily ban, or
permanently ban participants according to impact and repeated behavior.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
